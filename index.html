    Recontix

    Overview

    Transactions should be simply and easy, but most importantly, safe.

    Not restricting, but limiting transactions to within campus radius and official students will help students feel safer when reselling and exchanging concert tickets.

    Why?

    Los Angeles is the heart of entertainment & media, and more than ever, students are attending live concerts and events.

    Ticket exchange and reselling occurs often among students at USC, but many question the reliability and safety for these transactions.

    How?

    Recontix is a full-stack web/mobile app that will allow USC students to validate their profiles first through their USC unique ID numbers.

    We want students to feel safe and at ease when conducting risky transactions with strangers.

    Trade n' Exchange

    Overview

    Trade n' Exchange is a case study of a web/mobile app that focuses on safer and easier transactions for college students buying/selling used home goods and furniture.

    Why?

    A friend of mine got scammed on Facebook Marketplace when she was trying to buy a lamp for her bedroom. I saw this as unfortunate, but also unfair that desperate college students were being targetted.

    I wanted to take matters into my own hands, and created a product that limits listings to only students that are verified USC students.

    I bring inspiration directly from my own favorite product/platform, Depop and their amazing way of showing the buyer/seller their ratings and reviews from previous users who interacted with them.
